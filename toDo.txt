## Objective

1.Update the score as the snake eats a piece of food.
2.Update this information at the bottom of the snake viewport.


## Inputs Required

1. Latest Score.
2. Current updates of the Position of head and food.

## Procedure

Set the initial score as zero.
Display the initial score as zero at the bottom of the snake viewport.
Check if the snake has eaten the food or not after every move.
Increase the score by 10 as soon as the snake eats the food.
Update the score on the display Page.
Show the score on the display.
